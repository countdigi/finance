and should not be used to make your own financial decisions without consulting a professional first.

## FRS - Florida Retirement System Funds

- Total US Stock Market
  - FRS US Stock Market Index Fund (120)
    - Actually: IWV - iShares Russell 3000 ETF
- Total International Stock Market
  - FRS Foreign Stock Index Fund (200)
    - Actually: IXUS - iShares Core MSCI Total International ETF
- Total Bond Market
  - FRS US Bond Enhanced Index Fund (80)
    - Actually: AGG - iShares Core Total USD Bond Market ETF
