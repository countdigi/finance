# finance

## index funds

There are 6 main classes of passive index funds from a "Boglehead" perspective:
- S&P500
- Total US Stock Market
- Total International Stock market
- Total World Market (Total Stock Market + Total International Stock market)
- Total US Bond Market
- Total International Bond Market

The Vanguard Target-Retirement-Date funds are a "fund-of-funds" consisting of a mix of the following which adjust their bond allocation to a more conservative level as the fund approaches the retirement date.
- Total US Stock Market (TUSM)
- Total International Stock Market (TISM)
- Total US Bond Market (TUBM)
- Total International Bond Market (TIBM)
